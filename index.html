A Coffee Tutorial

One of my main passions in life is coffee-making. As a fun little addition to my portfolio, I figured I would share this passion with you via a video from one of my favorite online content creators! Don't worry about writing anything down; I've got you covered.
Note that this technique is for a v60 brewer with a ratio of 3 grams of coffee to 50 grams of water.

  1. Boil your water to about 100 degrees celsius
  2. Grind your coffee beans
  3. Pre-heat your v60 and rinse your filter paper
  4. Pour your grinds into the v60 and make a small hole in the middle
  5. Pour up to 50 grams, or 20% of your total water, onto the grinds to begin the blooming process, swirling once finished
  6. Let the coffee grounds bloom for about 45 seconds
  7. Pour in another 50 grams, or another 20%, making gentle circles with the water as you pour
  8. Pause for about 10 seconds
  9. Repeat this process until all 250 grams of water are used
  10. Give the brewer a gentle swirl and allow the coffee to draw down completely
